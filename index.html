Ingredients

  • 3 large egg whites, at room temperature
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/8 teaspoon cream of tartar
  • 3/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 cup of milk chocolate (optional)

Instructions

Step 1

In a stand mixer, add the egg whites, vanilla and cream of tartar. Beat until the mixture is frothy. Sift the powdered sugar and gradually add the sugar 1 tablespoon at a time, waiting about 15 seconds between each addition. Once all of the sugar has been added, beat for an additional 5 minutes. The egg whites should be glossy and stiff but not dry

Step 2

Preheat the oven to 200 degrees F; line 2 baking sheets with parchment paper.Spoon the mixture into a large pastry bag fitted with a large star tip. Pipe 2-inch cookies onto the lined baking sheets, leaving 1 inch between each cookie.

Step 3

Bake for 45 minutes, then turn off the oven and leave the cookies in the oven for an additional 30 minutes. Remove and cool completely
